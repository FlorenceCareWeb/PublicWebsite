Trusted Care Staffing for UK Care Homes

Connecting qualified healthcare professionals with care homes, nursing homes and supported living providers across the UK.

Our Mission

Florence Care 24 LTD specialises in medical and social care recruitment, supplying high-quality staff to care homes and health settings across the UK. Our mission is to match reliable professionals with employers who demand excellence and compliance.

We focus on permanent, temporary and contract staffing solutions for nursing, care and support roles, ensuring every placement is safe, trained and compliant.

Our Services

Care Home Staffing

Experienced nurses, care assistants and support staff supplied to residential, nursing and dementia care settings.

Temporary & Permanent Recruitment

Flexible staffing solutions for short-term cover, long-term placements and permanent roles.

Compliance & Vetting

All candidates are DBS checked, referenced and assessed to meet regulatory workforce standards.

Employer Support

Dedicated account management, workforce planning and retention support for care providers.

Core Recruitment Focus

Registered Nurses

Skilled nurse placements for nursing homes, hospitals and specialist care providers.

Care Assistants

Qualified support workers and healthcare assistants for residential and domiciliary care.

Rehab & Support Staff

Experienced professionals for rehabilitation, dementia and specialist social care settings.
