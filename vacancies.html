Registered Nurse

Location: Care homes across the UK

Contract: Full-time / Part-time / Bank shifts

Provide clinical nursing support, manage medication, and contribute to personalised care plans in residential settings.

  • Deliver high-quality nursing care within residential and nursing home environments.
  • Support care planning, medication administration and clinical record keeping.
  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to promote compliant, compassionate care.

Requirements: NMC registration, care home experience preferred, strong communication skills.

Benefits: Flexible shifts, mentoring support, competitive pay and ongoing development.

Health Care Assistant

Location: Residential and nursing care settings

Contract: Permanent, temporary or flexible shifts

Help residents with personal care, mobility and daily living tasks while working with nursing teams.

  • Support residents with personal care, mobility and daily living activities.
  • Work closely with nurses and care teams to deliver compassionate support.
  • Help maintain a safe, caring environment for people with complex needs.

Requirements: Previous care experience preferred, strong teamwork and a positive attitude.

Benefits: Flexible hours, training opportunities and a supportive employer network.
